WHO WE ARE

Our Story

Student Founder

Hi, I am Amogh, the student founder of NanoConstruct AI. I believe the construction industry deserves the same technological revolution that transformed transportation, manufacturing and communication. Rather than relying solely on labour-intensive construction methods, I envision a future where Artificial Intelligence coordinates autonomous construction robots to build stronger, safer and more sustainable homes.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the global construction industry by combining AI, robotics and advanced materials into one intelligent construction platform. Our vision is to dramatically reduce construction time, lower costs, improve worker safety and make quality housing more accessible.
